In MS-Word, a paragraph is any amount of text, graphics, object or other items that are followed by a paragraph mark. A paragraph mark is inserted each time while pressing the ENTER key.

In order to change the formatting of a paragraph, select the paragraph and then apply the formats you wish to apply. You can format a paragraph by using one of the following methods:

  1. Right click on paragraph, click on “Paragraph” option in context menu
  2. Place the cursor within the paragraph
    1. Select Home > Paragraph subtask menu
    2. Click on the down arrow on the lower right side of the subtask menu. This will display the following menu for you to choose the paragraph formatting.
