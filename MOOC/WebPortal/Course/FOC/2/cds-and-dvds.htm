Compact Disk (CD)

Compact disc, or CD for short, is a digital optical disc data storage format. The format was originally developed to store and play back sound recordings. It generally contains capacity of 700 MB.

CD


DVD

DVD is a digital optical disc storage format. DVDs offer higher storage capacity than compact discs while having the same dimensions. DVD can store data around 4.7 GB. DVDs are used in DVD-Video consumer digital video format and in DVD-Audio consumer digital audio format.
