This is the address that identifies a computer on a network using TCP/IP. An IP address contains series of four numbers unique to the computer concerned e.g.: 90.399.424.34. This address is usually supplied by an Internet Service Provider (ISP)

E.g. your house has specific address. This address is assigned by the Municipal Corporation of your city. Any one wants to reach your house, must know the complete address. Similarly in computer networks, each computer has unique IP address.
