PowerPoint consists of multiple views to help you in the creation and presentation of your slides. These views are Normal, Slide Sorter, and Slide Show. The first two views are for creating your presentation and the last one is for presentation of slide show.

You can access these views by clicking the respective view buttons at the bottom right of the PowerPoint window. Or select the View and then the respective command on the menu bar. For example, if you want to choose Normal view, select View > Normal command on the menu bar.
