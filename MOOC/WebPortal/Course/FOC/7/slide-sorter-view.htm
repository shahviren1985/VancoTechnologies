A small image of each slide is displayed on Slide Sorter View. Slides can easily be ordered and sorted using this view. You can also use this view to add special effect, such as the transactions that occur when each new slide appears. To access Slide Sorter view, click on Slide Sorter view button at the bottom left of the window or select View > Slide Sorter command on the menu bar.
