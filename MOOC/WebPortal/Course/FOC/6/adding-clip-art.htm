ClipArt is a single media file, including sound, animation, art or movie. Steps to insert a ClipArt :

  1. Click on Insert Tab.
  2. From Illustrations Group, Click on Clip Art. Then Select a Collection and press Go Button
  3. Click on a clip from the collection. Clip will be inserted in the worksheet.
  4. You also have an option to search for a specific Clip Art.

  5. To edit the clip, click on the clip. This will display Format tab. Choose the relevant group from the ribbon of Format tab and do the necessary changes.
