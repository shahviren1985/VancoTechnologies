Formulas are entries that have an equation that calculates the value to display. We DO NOT type in the numbers we are looking for; we type in the equation with reference to different data present in excel sheet. This equation will be updated upon the change or entry of any data that is referenced in the equation.

Resizable formula bar : To prevent the formulas to cover the other data in worksheet, the formula bar automatically resizes to accommodate complex, long formulas.
