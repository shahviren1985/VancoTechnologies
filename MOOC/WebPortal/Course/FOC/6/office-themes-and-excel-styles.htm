By the help of a specific style, in Excel , the data can be quickly formatted in the worksheet by the help of a theme. You can share themes across other releases of Office e.g. Word , Power point .

Themes

A theme is defined as a predefined set of colors, lines, fonts and fills effects. Themes are used to make great-looking documents.

Styles

Styles can be applied to change the appearance of Excel charts, tables, PivotTables, diagrams or shapes. It can be customized to meet user specific requirements.
