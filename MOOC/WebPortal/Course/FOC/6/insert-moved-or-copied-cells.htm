  1. Select the cell or range of cells that contains the data that you want to copy.
  2. Press CTRL+X, if you want to CUT the cell content. Press CTRL+C, if you want to COPY the content of cells.
  3. Right-click the upper-left cell of the paste area, and then clicks Insert Cut Cells or Insert Copied Cells on the shortcut menu.
  4. In the Insert Paste dialog box, click the direction in which you want to shift the surrounding cells.
