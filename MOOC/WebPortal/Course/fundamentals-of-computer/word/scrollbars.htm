The typing area is bordered on the right side by the vertical scroll bar with a scroll button and arrows. You can scroll the page by either of following options :

  • Click on arrow button present on the scroll bar (up arrow button or down arrow button)
  • Scroll up/down using scroll button present on mouse

In Word 2007, the options such as view documents and zoom are also available on the bottom bar for easy access.
