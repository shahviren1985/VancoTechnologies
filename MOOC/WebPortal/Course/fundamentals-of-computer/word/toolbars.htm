Word 2007 provides a customized quick access toolbar to organize the tools available for easy and fast access of the commands. Toolbars can be customized by following the steps:

  1. Click on Office button on top left corner.
  2. Click on “Word Options” button at the bottom of the menu
  3. Click on “Customize” option on left navigation
  4. Make necessary changes and click on “Ok” button
