Google search engine have few hidden tricks which are fun to use. Sometimes these tricks help us to get quick results. Some of these tricks are listed below :


Define

To get the definition of any word, you can directly type in define followed by word. E.g. define: cricket


Calculator

Basic and complex arithmetic operations could be directly performed on Google calculator. E.g. calculator


Distance between

You can get the distance between two locations very easily. Enter two location names in search box. E.g. distance between Mumbai and Pune


Do a barrel roll

Your entire page will be rotated 360 degree. E.g. do a barrel roll


Weather in

You can get a weather of any location. E.g. weather in Mumbai


Tilt

Your search result page will be tilted little bit. E.g. tilt


Zerg rush

E.g. zerg rush
