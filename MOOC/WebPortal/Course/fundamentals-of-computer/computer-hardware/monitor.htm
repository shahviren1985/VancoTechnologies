Monitor is the most important output device because people interact with this device most intensively. Information processed within the computer processor, that needs to be visually displayed, is sent to video adapter. The video adapter converts information from the format used, in the same manner as a television displays information sent to it by a cable service.

Different monitor types are as follows :

  1. Cathode Ray Tube (CRT)
  2. Liquid Crystal Displays (LCD)
  3. LED

Cathode Ray Tube (CRT) : CRT is the typical monitor that you see on a desktop computer. It looks a lot like a television screen, and works the same way. This type uses a large vacuum tube, called cathode ray tube (CRT). Nowadays, these monitors are considered outdated.

Liquid Crystal Displays (LCD) : This type of monitors is also known as flat panel monitor. Most of these employ liquid crystal displays (LCDs) to render images. These days LCD monitor are very popular. When people talk about the capabilities of various monitors, one critical statistic is the resolution of the monitor.

LED : An LED display is a flat panel display, which uses light-emitting diodes as a video display.
