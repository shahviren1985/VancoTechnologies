Windows XP help and support is great tool to learn Windows operating systems. User can get a help on specific topic or on current task, which is being executed.

To start help :

  1. Click on the Windows start button
  2. Click on “Help and Support” option

A list of help and support topics and online tutorial will be displayed on the screen. User can use the tabs in the screen to search for the desired information.

Help and Support options


  1. 1. Click on a topic or task to know more about or type in a search word to locate the help on a specific topic
  2. 2. Click on the ‘Index’ tab to locate specific topic listed alphabetically and then follow the instructions displayed on the screen
  3. 3. Click on ‘Favorites’ tab to add Help Topics, search results to your Favorites list to make them easy to locate in the future
  4. 4. Click on ‘History’ to pick from a list of Help and Support pages that you have read in the past
  5. 5. Click on ‘Support’ to get various kind of online support such as Microsoft online support, News Group on Windows etc.
  6. 6. Click on ‘Option’ to configure the help and support center as per your requirement

To close Help and Support windows, select the close button (Χ) in the upper-right corner of the window.
