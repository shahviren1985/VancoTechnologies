To enter data into your worksheet you must first have a cell or range selected. When you open an Excel worksheet, cell A1 is already active. An active cell will appear to have a darker border around it than other cells on the worksheet.

The simplest way to select a cell is with your mouse pointer. Move your mouse to the desired cell and click on it with left button. Whatever you type goes into the cell.

To select a range of cells, click on one cell, hold down the left mouse button and drag the mouse pointer to the last cell of the range you want to select.
