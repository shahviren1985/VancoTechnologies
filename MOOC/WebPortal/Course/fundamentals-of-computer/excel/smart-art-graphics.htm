Visual representation of information and ideas is called “Smart Art” Graphics. They can be used to quickly, easily and effectively communicate a message. To create a Smart Art graphic, you have to choose a type for example, Process, Cycle, Hierarchy or Relationship. Steps to Create a Smart Art :

  1. Click on Insert Tab.
  2. From Illustrations Group, Click on Smart Art

    3. Insert SmartArt Graphics


  3. Choose a category (Say Hierarchy) and click OK.
  4. To enter values just click on the component where you want to enter text and write the text.

    5. choose smart graphics


You can apply different effects on the SmartArt, by the help of Design tab. Click on SmartArt. The Design tab will be visible with its ribbon. Select appropriate group from the ribbon and apply the desired effects.
