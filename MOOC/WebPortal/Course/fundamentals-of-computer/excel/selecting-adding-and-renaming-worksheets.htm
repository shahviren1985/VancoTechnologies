The worksheets in a workbook are accessible by clicking the worksheet tabs just above the status bar. By default, three worksheets are included in each workbook. One can add more worksheet in a workbook.


Excel Worksheets


  • To quickly insert a new worksheet at the end of existing worksheets Click the Insert Worksheet tab as shown in above figure.
  • To insert a new worksheet before an existing worksheet, select the worksheet before which you want to insert a new worksheet. Follow steps :
    1. Select Home tab
    2. Click cells Group
    3. Click Insert
    4. Click Insert Sheet
  • To rename a worksheet, follow these steps :
    1. Right click on the worksheet tab which you want to rename
    2. Select rename from the Pop Up menu
    3. Type new name for the Worksheet
