Hide option hides the selected column(s) or row(s), though the data is still there. Hiding column(s) or row(s) will help you to prevent unwanted changes. To hide a column, follow these steps :


hide columns


Select the column you want to hide by clicking on the column header. Say you want to hide column C, Right Click on the Column to hide and click on the Hide option.
