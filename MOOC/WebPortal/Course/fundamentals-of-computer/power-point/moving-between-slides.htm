You can move from one slide to another slide by using the following tools :


Scroll Bars

Use the Scroll Bars to move from one slide to another slide or through the text that is displayed in the notes pane and the outline pane.


Next Slide and Previous Slide Buttons

The Next Slide and Previous Slide buttons move you to the next slide or previous slide in a slide show. These buttons, indicated by a double-arrow, are located at the bottom of the vertical scroll bar.


options for moving between sliders


Using Outline Pane: Change to Outline view and then click on the slide that you want to view. You can move around in the outline pane by using your mouse, or by using up and down arrow keys.
