The Slide Show View is useful to preview your presentation to make sure everything is in place, or when you deliver a presentation to an audience. Because all sounds, animations, hyperlinks, and action settings of your presentation will be working, you can see exactly how your show will turn out. To access Slide Show view, click on Slide Show button at the bottom left of the window or select View > Slide Show command on the menu bar.
